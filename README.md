# 📱 Smart Phones Market Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 1,020+ smartphone models — pricing, RAM, battery, and rating trends across brands.

## Why I built this

I wanted to actually know what drives a phone's rating instead of guessing — RAM? Battery? Price? So I took a smartphone dataset, cleaned it up, and built a dashboard to dig out the real patterns.

## What's inside

- **Brand-wise count & price value** — who's shipping the most models, and who's actually making the most money per model
- **Price distribution** — where most phones in the market really sit
- **RAM distribution & rating trend** — does more RAM mean a better-rated phone? (Short answer: yes — the trend line makes it obvious)
- **Battery analysis** — most common battery capacities across the dataset
- **Top 10 highest-rated phones**
- **Top 10 most expensive phones**
- Fully filterable by **Brand, Price Range, RAM, and OS**

## Key insights

- 1,020 phone models, averaging ₹31,372, with an overall average rating of 3.91/5
- Samsung & Xiaomi lead in model count (135 each), but Apple's total price value is the second-highest despite far fewer models
- Average rating drops steadily as RAM decreases — 12GB phones rate noticeably higher than 2GB phones
- Highest-rated phone: Huawei Mate Xs 2 (rating score: 89)
- Most expensive phone: Vertu Signature Touch (₹6,50,000)

## Tools used

- Power BI — data modeling, DAX, dashboard design
- Excel / Power Query — data cleaning
